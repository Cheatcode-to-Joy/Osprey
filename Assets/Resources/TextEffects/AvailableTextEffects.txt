Custom Commands:

- {split} # Splits the following text into its separate dialogue entry.
- {hold} # Waits for player input to progress.
- {pause duration=1.0} # Pauses dialogue progression.
- {speed multiplier=1.0} # Modifies the speed of dialogue progression.
